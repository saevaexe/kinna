Kinna

A parenting companion for the early years. Science-backed milestones, vaccination tracking, daily care logs, and age-based guidance — local-first and privacy-conscious by design.

What is Kinna?

Kinna helps new parents navigate the crucial first years with confidence. Track your baby's development, stay on top of vaccinations, log daily feeding and sleep patterns, and receive personalized guidance based on your child's age.

Our content is based on WHO guidelines and T.C. Ministry of Health protocols. All data stays on your device — no servers, no tracking, no compromise on your family's privacy.

Features

  • Developmental milestone tracking (0-24 months)
  • TR vaccination schedule with reminders
  • Daily care logging (feeding, sleep, diaper, notes)
  • Growth tracking (weight & height)
  • Food introduction & allergy log
  • Age-based safety alerts
  • Mother, father, and caregiver personas

Privacy First

Most of the data you enter — your baby's name, health logs, vaccination records, and notes — stays on your device. Subscription purchase verification is handled by Apple and RevenueCat. No third-party analytics SDKs, no ads, and no cloud backend for your baby data.
